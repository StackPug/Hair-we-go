At Hair We Go, we are passionate about transforming hair and boosting confidence. Based in Essex, our skilled stylists bring years of salon and mobile experience to every appointment — whether you visit us or we come to you.

From a fresh trim to a full colour transformation, every client receives a personalised service in a relaxed, welcoming environment. No rush, no judgement — just great hair.
